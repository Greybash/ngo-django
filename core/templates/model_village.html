Model Village

A Vision for Sustainable Rural Transformation

Evergreen Villages Trust envisions rural communities where development is holistic, inclusive, and sustainable. The Model Village initiative is a long-term approach that strengthens social systems, economic resilience, cultural identity, and environmental balance.

Rather than isolated interventions, this model focuses on building self-reliant communities driven by participation, dignity, and shared responsibility.

Beginning with V. Mangammalpatti, this initiative serves as a scalable blueprint for villages across India.

Health & Wellbeing

A Foundation for Strong Communities

Health forms the foundation of a productive and confident village. The Model Village ensures accessible primary healthcare with special attention to women, children, senior citizens, and vulnerable individuals.

Preventive care and awareness programs on nutrition, hygiene, and mental wellbeing empower villagers to make informed health choices.

Education & Youth Engagement

Nurturing Future Leaders

Education in the Model Village goes beyond academics, integrating values, discipline, life skills, and leadership development inspired by the Gurukulam philosophy.

Digital learning and skill-based education prepare rural youth for modern opportunities while remaining rooted in their communities.

Healthcare Services

Free Medical Camps & Community Wellness

Evergreen Villages Trust organized four free medical camps in V. Mangammalpatti, delivering essential checkups, diagnoses, and treatments to the community. Continuous follow-ups ensured sustained health outcomes and long-term impact.

365 Days of Compassionate Service

Nutritious meals were served every day of the year to elderly and needy individuals, fostering dignity, compassion, and care while strengthening community bonds. This initiative ensures that no one in our community goes without nourishment.
